# sources.md — Land Value Tax for New Zealand

*One entry per source ref used in `findings.md`. Reliability note per source.
Strong = primary/official/peer-reviewed; Medium = reputable secondary/expert
synthesis; Weak = single blog/advocacy/anecdote.*

---

## Strong (primary / official / peer-reviewed)

- **QV** — Quotable Value, "About rating valuations" / rating revaluation pages.
  Official valuer for NZ council rating; defines Land Value vs Capital Value.
  https://www.qv.co.nz/about/about-rating-valuations/ — *strong (official).*
- **BullerDC** — Buller District Council, "Rating valuations." Council-level
  confirmation of the LV/CV split and 3-yearly revaluation.
  https://bullerdc.govt.nz/property-rates-and-building/rating-valuations/ — *strong (official).*
- **StatsNZ** — Stats NZ, "The current state of housing in Aotearoa New Zealand."
  Official housing/income figures.
  https://www.stats.govt.nz/news/the-current-state-of-housing-in-aotearoa-new-zealand/ — *strong (official statistics).*
- **Demographia2024** — Demographia International Housing Affordability 2024.
  Median-multiple dataset and the 3.0 affordability threshold.
  http://www.demographia.com/dhi.pdf — *strong (dataset; note: author Wendell Cox has a pro-sprawl stance — methodology is transparent).*
- **IRD** — Inland Revenue, "The bright-line test." Statutory description of NZ
  property-gain rules. https://www.ird.govt.nz/property/buying-and-selling/when-you-need-to-pay/the-brightline-test — *strong (statutory).*
- **TWG-FinalReport** — Tax Working Group, "Future of Tax: Final Report Vol I
  (Recommendations)," Feb 2019. Recommendation that a land tax could be a local
  (council) tax. https://taxworkinggroup.govt.nz/resources/future-tax-final-report-vol-i-html.html — *strong (official report).*
- **TeAra-Taxes** — Te Ara, Encyclopedia of New Zealand, "Taxes." Rating bases
  (land/annual/capital value); national land tax history.
  https://teara.govt.nz/en/taxes/print — *strong (official encyclopedia).*
- **Govt.nz-defer** — govt.nz, "Postpone or defer paying your rates." Official
  description of NZ rates postponement (defer until sale/estate).
  https://www.govt.nz/browse/housing-and-property/getting-help-with-housing/postpone-defer-paying-rates/ — *strong (official).*
- **QLDC** — Queenstown-Lakes District Council, rates postponement info sheet
  for over-65s. https://www.qldc.govt.nz/services/rates-property/rates-rebates-remission-postponement — *strong (official scheme).*
- **Napier** — Napier City Council, "Postponement and remission."
  https://www.napier.govt.nz/services/properties-and-rates/rates/postponement-and-remission/ — *strong (official scheme).*
- **OAG-2006** — Office of the Auditor-General, "Rates postponement" report.
  Independent official review of the schemes.
  https://oag.parliament.nz/2006/rates-postponement/part2.htm — *strong (official audit).*
- **ChicagoFed** — Federal Reserve Bank of Chicago, "Land Value Taxes—What They
  Are and Where They Come From" (2023). No-deadweight-loss reasoning; Smith
  attribution. https://www.chicagofed.org/publications/chicago-fed-letter/2023/489 — *strong (central-bank publication).*
- **IMF-WP-2022** — IMF Working Paper, "Equity and Efficiency Effects of Land
  Value Taxation" (2022). https://www.imf.org/-/media/Files/Publications/WP/2022/English/wpiea2022263-print-pdf.ashx — *strong (working paper).*
- **NBER-w8913 / Springer** — "The Property Tax as a Tax on Value: Deadweight
  Loss." Peer-reviewed treatment showing real assessments aren't perfectly
  efficient. https://www.nber.org/papers/w8913 — *strong (peer-reviewed); used for caveat C2.*

- **LincolnInst** — Lincoln Institute of Land Policy, "Land Value Taxation:
  Theory, Evidence, and Practice." https://www.lincolninst.edu/app/uploads/legacy-files/pubfiles/land-value-taxation-chp.pdf — *medium-to-strong (specialist institute; has an interest in the topic — disclosed).*
- **LocalHousingSolutions** — "Land value taxation" policy library entry.
  https://www.localhousingsolutions.org/housing-policy-library/land-value-taxation/ — *medium.*
- **Sightline** — Sightline Institute, "To Revitalize Downtowns, Tax Land
  Speculation." https://www.sightline.org/2014/06/10/to-revitalize-downtowns-tax-land-speculation/ — *medium (advocacy think-tank — pro-LVT stake disclosed).*
- **arXiv-2511.21766** — "The dynamic of a tax on land value: concepts, models
  and impact scenario." https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.21766 — *medium (preprint, not yet peer-reviewed).*
- **ExpatFocus** — "New Zealand – Property Taxes." Secondary confirmation of the
  1992 land-tax/estate-duty abolition. https://www.expatfocus.com/new-zealand/guide/new-zealand-property-taxes — *medium.*
- **Retirement.govt.nz-research** — "Equitable Rates Relief" (Duyen Tran, 2023),
  research hosted by the Retirement Commission. Notes low uptake of relief
  schemes. https://assets.retirement.govt.nz/public/Uploads/Research/2023/Duyen-Tran_TAAO_Equitable-Rates-Relief.pdf — *medium (commissioned research); used for caveat C3.*
- **Albemarle / Multnomah / Instead** — US use-value / deferral programmes for
  farm and forest land, illustrating deferral as an established tool.
  https://www.albemarle.org/government/finance/land-use ;
  https://www.multco.us/assessment-taxation/deferral-programs-agricultural-land-and-natural-spaces ;
  https://www.instead.com/resources/blog/farmland-sellers-can-pay-capital-gains-tax-over-4-years — *medium (official US programmes) / weak (the Instead blog).*
